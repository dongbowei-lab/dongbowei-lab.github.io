Principal Investigator

Dr. Dong Bowei

Research Scientist of IME
NRF Fellow

Bowei Dong received his Bachelor of Science in Physics and Mathematics from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2015, and his Doctor of Philosophy in Electrical Engineering from the National University of Singapore in 2019. He spent two years at the University of Oxford, UK as a postdoctoral fellow. His doctoral thesis focused on mid-infrared integrated photonics, and his postdoctoral research centred on photonic computing. In 2023, he returned to Singapore to work as a Senior Scientist and Principal Investigator at the Institute of Microelectronics (IME).
